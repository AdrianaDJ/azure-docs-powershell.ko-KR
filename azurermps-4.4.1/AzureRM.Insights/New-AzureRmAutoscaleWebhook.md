# New-AzureRmAutoscaleWebhook

## SYNOPSIS

```
New-AzureRmAutoscaleWebhook [-ServiceUri] <String> [[-Properties] <Hashtable>]
 [-DefaultProfile <IAzureContextContainer>] [<CommonParameters>]
```
